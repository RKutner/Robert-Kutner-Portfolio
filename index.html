About Me

I was introduced to technology at a very young age and have always been curious about how things work. “Checking under the hood” so to speak… While my career took a different turn, I did notice that I was still everyone’s “go-to guy” for tech questions and solving problems. It became apparent to me that I had developed a keen ability to problem solve and streamline processes using technology. I decided to explore this a bit further…

Fast forward to the University of California, Berkeley Extension Web Development Bootcamp where I completed a full-time, intensive, 12-week program where I became proficient in JavaScript, HTML, SQL, CSS, Node, Express, MongoDB, React.js, MySQL, and NoSQL. I enjoy putting things together, like pieces of a puzzle; seeing how things interact and ultimately creating a visually appealing experience for the user/viewer.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy musical theatre, singing, baking, and strategy-based board games. I am also a devoted servant to my two house cats, Frisbee and Lili.
